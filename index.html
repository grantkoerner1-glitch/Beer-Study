    About / Vibe

    A bottle shop heart with a living-room soul.

    Beer Study was built for people who like flavor, conversation, and discovering something new without feeling quizzed at the bar. Ask for a wild sour, a clean lager, a natural wine, or a no-proof can that still tastes like a real choice.

    The room is eclectic by design: warm light, odd little details, playlists that never take themselves too seriously, and staff who know what they're pouring.

    Tap List / Drink Experience

    Rotating on purpose, not for show.

    The wall moves fast: local standouts, cult favorites, and style diversity that keeps regulars curious.
