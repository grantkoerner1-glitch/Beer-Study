    About Beer Study

    Built for curious drinkers, casual sippers, and everyone in between.

    We started Beer Study to give Durham a beer spot with personality: equal parts bottle shop, taproom, and community living room. Come geek out over a saison, discover your new easy-drinker, or just post up with friends and let the night unfold.

    Our team keeps the draft wall fresh, the fridges stacked with standout picks, and the energy welcoming from first visit to fiftieth. No gatekeeping. No pretension. Just good pours and good people.
